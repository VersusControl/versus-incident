## Why teams choose Versus

- **Self-hosted by default** — your logs never leave your infrastructure. The wedge for
  regulated buyers in EU fintech, health, and gov.
- **Upstream of on-call, not a replacement** — Versus is the AI detection +
  noise-reduction layer that *feeds* [PagerDuty](./oncall/upstream-of-on-call.md),
  Opsgenie, and incident.io, cutting page volume instead of adding another rotation.
- **Deterministic and auditable** — drain miner, EWMA, and grace periods are
  explainable; every AI decision is recorded. Regulated teams can only buy what
  they can audit.
- **Open core, not open bait** — the MIT core stays production-grade for free,
  forever.
