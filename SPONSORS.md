# Sponsor Versus Incident

Versus Incident is MIT-licensed and developed in the open. It is built and
maintained on nights and weekends. Sponsorships pay for maintenance time,
hosting for the demo environment, CI minutes, and faster issue response.

If your team relies on Versus in production — or saves money by self-hosting
instead of paying per-seat for a commercial incident tool — please consider
sponsoring.

## How to sponsor

- **GitHub Sponsors** — https://github.com/sponsors/versuscontrol
- **Support contracts & paid integrations** — email
  `admin@versusincident.com` (SLA, private support channel, custom
  channels/sources, on-prem deployment help).

## Sponsorship tiers

> **Sponsorship is not the commercial product.** Versus has one paid commercial
> tier today — **Versus Enterprise** (self-hosted commercial license) — which
> delivers organization-scaling features (multi-tenancy, SSO, RBAC, audit). A
> hosted **Versus Cloud** on the same binary is a *future* offering and is not
> sold yet. Sponsorship below is **community funding for the MIT core**: it keeps
> self-hosting free and well-maintained. If you need org-scaling features or a
> commercial relationship, see [Commercial tier](#commercial-tier-enterprise) —
> not the sponsor tiers. Sponsoring buys goodwill and visibility, never
> closed-source carve-outs.

The recommended sponsor tiers are aligned with the commercial pricing story so
the two ladders read coherently and never compete:

| Tier | Amount (USD / month) | Aligned with | Benefits |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Backer** | $25 | Individual self-hoster | Name listed in `SPONSORS.md` |
| **Bronze** | $100 | Small team self-hosting the core | Logo in `README.md` (small) + `SPONSORS.md` |
| **Silver** | $500 | Mid team self-hosting the core | Logo in `README.md` (medium) + priority issue triage + private Discord channel |
| **Gold** | $2,000 | Org running the core at scale (≈ Enterprise Starter interest) | Logo in `README.md` (large) + monthly roadmap call (1h) + priority feature review |
| **Platinum** | Custom | Strategic backer / pre-commercial enterprise | Everything above + named integration ("Datadog support sponsored by …") + co-marketing |

Sponsor amounts deliberately sit **alongside, not on top of**, the commercial
price points: a self-hoster who would otherwise scale into Versus Enterprise can
instead fund the core at a comparable level and keep running it themselves.
Sponsorship benefits are **community/visibility perks** and never include the
license-gated Enterprise features — those are sold, not sponsored.

One-time sponsorships are welcome and acknowledged in `SPONSORS.md` for the
calendar quarter.

## What sponsorship does **not** buy

- A guaranteed feature ship date — we discuss and prioritise, but
  engineering capacity is finite.
- Closed-source carve-outs. Everything sponsors fund stays MIT-licensed.
- Removal of competing references. The project will continue to document
  comparisons honestly.

## Commercial tier (Enterprise)

If you need an organization-scaling product rather than a way to fund the OSS
core, **Versus Enterprise** is the commercial tier. It is priced on **monitored
services, never per-seat**, and billed **monthly**:

- **Versus Enterprise** — self-hosted commercial license for multi-tenant org
  isolation, SSO/SCIM, RBAC + audit, per-org model gateway, and air-gapped
  supply-chain packaging. **Enterprise Starter** list **$1,500/mo** →
  Enterprise ($3,500/mo) → Plus ($7,000/mo+).

### Founding-Customer Beta — Enterprise Starter at $199/mo

**Get in before we're well-known.** We're opening the **Enterprise Starter**
tier to the **first 10 founding customers** at an introductory self-hosted beta
price. Low-risk first price, full features, locked in — and a transparent path
to list price later.

> **Enterprise Starter:** ~~$1,500/mo list~~ → **$199/mo founding-customer
> beta** — first **10 customers only**, price **locked at $199/mo for 12
> months**.

- **Full feature set, no asterisks.** Founding customers run the **complete
  Enterprise Starter feature set — same binary, same features**. The discount is
  on **price, not capability**; nothing is feature-clipped to reach $199/mo.
- **Price-locked for 12 months** from signup, then a **published, honest
  conversion path** — no surprise re-rate:
  - **Year 2** — $750/mo (founding-loyalty rate)
  - **Year 3+** — $1,500/mo (Enterprise Starter list)
  - We send a **60-day pre-renewal notice** before any step-up.
- **Why act now.** The cohort is capped at **10 customers** and the **12-month
  lock** is only available to founding signups. Once the 10 seats are filled,
  Starter is list price for everyone else.

This is the **self-hosted Versus Enterprise beta**. It is **not** the deferred,
managed **Versus Cloud** tier — the Cloud "Team" price point is a separate
future offering that is not sold today.

> A hosted **Versus Cloud** (managed, usage-based SaaS) is a **future** offering
> built on the same binary. It is **not sold today** — we are shipping and selling
> Versus Enterprise first. The architecture is kept Cloud-ready so the hosted
> tier can launch without reworking the core.

Versus Enterprise is the **same Go binary** as the OSS core with a license
check that flips on the additive org-scaling features. Nothing in the MIT core is
gated behind it — and the **founding-customer discount applies only to the
commercial Enterprise tier**; no OSS capability is paid, time-boxed, or behind
the beta. See the open-core commitment in [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md).

## For companies: paid options instead of sponsorship

If "sponsoring open source" doesn't fit your procurement, and you don't yet need
the Enterprise tier above, these OSS-aligned options usually fit:

1. **Annual support contract** — SLA on issues, private support channel,
   install/upgrade help. From $6,000/year.
2. **Paid integration** — your monitoring stack, your message channel, your
   on-call provider. Built into the OSS project, MIT-licensed. From $4,000.
3. **On-prem / air-gapped deployment package** — Helm chart hardening,
   security review support, signed images. Quote on request. *(For
   license-gated air-gapped Enterprise features, see Versus Enterprise above.)*

Email `admin@versusincident.com` with your use case.
